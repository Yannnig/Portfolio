Our team of three dedicated students has successfully developed an immersive 3D Unity Escape Game. Throughout the project, we invested a total of 40 hours per person, crafting intricate puzzles, designing captivating environments, and implementing engaging gameplay mechanics. The result is an enthralling escape experience that promises to challenge player's minds while delivering a seamless and visually stunning adventure.
